Intranet news, published 23.2. 
Simplified business processes for all Scanfil sites
Scanfil is in a strategic growth phase, increasing the number of sites and employees rapidly. Our old sites* and new sites** have existing processes and certifications in place and run established management systems.
To support our company’s growth and key business processes, we have been designing a scalable and simplified framework which would allow smooth transition of new sites as part of Scanfil. This way, we will protect what is strategically important and stay agile, while maintaining the autonomy and strengths of each site. 
By identifying key business processes that need to be followed by all sites, we ensure they are aligned across the old and new Scanfil sites.
12 processes identified as the most critical for old and new sites
We have identified 38 business processes that should be implemented across all Scanfil sites. Of these, top 12 were identified as the most critical processes, which have now been simplified and updated. The rest, 26 processes, will be simplified by the first half of 2026.
Old sites must ensure that they follow the updated processes which will be released in Scanfil Management System (SMS). They are planned to be released in SMS by the end of February.
New sites must implement the top 12 processes at the first phase on the integration or according to their local business plan. The remaining 26 processes are to be implemented according to each integration project’s internal needs. 
The certification processes for old sites are not affected. The new sites will continue to manage certifications locally or can optionally join the group certification process where it is seen fit.
 
Top 12 Processes
-	Code of Conduct
-	Supplier Code of Conduct
-	IT/IS Security Policy
-	Group Authorization Manual
-	Finance Manual
-	Transfer Pricing Policy
-	R12 Month Forecasting
-	Security Incident and Breach Handling Process
-	Accidents and Incidents Handling
-	Disclosure and Communications Policy
-	Customer Agreement Policy
-	Treasury Policy
Next steps for the top 12 processes
The top 12 processes will be updated in SMS by February 26, 2026.
For old sites, we will kick off the implementation of updated processes in Quality and Performance Council meetings and functional meetings, schedule process training updates for site management teams, and launch virtual trainings for each process. Managing Directors will have the responsibility to cascade the process in their respective plants. 
For new sites, we will also initiate in-depth process trainings with SRXGlobal sites in the spring and continue with other new sites at a later stage according to the business plan led by the integration manager. 
Next steps for the remaining 26 key processes
The remaining 26 processes will be updated in SMS by the first half of 2026.
For old sites, the update will follow the normal process where process owners give training on updated processes.
New sites will implement the 26 processes according to their site-specific business plan led by the integration manager.
